Common Questions About AI Phone Answering

Straight answers to your concerns.

How fast does the AI respond to calls?
Under 800 milliseconds—faster than most human receptionists. Callers experience a natural conversation without awkward delays or "please hold" messages. The AI handles multiple calls simultaneously, so no one gets a busy signal.
Can it handle emergency calls?
Absolutely. We train the AI to identify true emergencies (flooding, no heat in winter, etc.) versus routine calls. Emergencies are immediately routed to your on-call technician with full context. The AI can even call multiple numbers until someone answers.
What if the AI doesn't understand the caller?
The AI is trained on thousands of trade business conversations and understands industry terminology. But if it encounters something truly unusual, it can seamlessly transfer the call to you with full context of the conversation so far. You can also review all calls and teach the AI new responses.
Does it integrate with my CRM/Software?
Yes. We integrate with ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, Service Fusion, and most major trade CRMs. The AI automatically creates customer records, books appointments, and logs call details. If you use something else, we can usually build a custom integration.
How long does setup take?
About 1-2 weeks from kickoff to go-live. You spend about 2 hours total: one 30-min call to share your services/pricing, and one 30-min call to review and approve the AI's training. We handle all the technical work, integrations, and testing.
What's the cost?
We start with a Free AI Revenue Audit to see if this makes sense for your business. Pricing varies based on your specific needs and complexity. Contact us for a custom quote. Most clients recover the investment with just 1-2 captured leads.

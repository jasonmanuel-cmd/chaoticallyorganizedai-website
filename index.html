What is SEO, GEO, and AEO?

SEO

Search Engine Optimization

The traditional practice of optimizing your website to rank higher on Google and other search engines. Includes keywords, meta tags, site speed, mobile optimization, and backlinks.

Why it matters: Google still drives the majority of web traffic. If you don't rank, you don't get found.

GEO

Generative Engine Optimization

Optimizing your content so AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini) can understand and recommend your business when users ask questions.

Why it matters: More people are asking AI "Who's the best plumber in Bakersfield?" instead of Googling. If your site isn't AI-ready, you're invisible.

AEO

Answer Engine Optimization

Structuring your content to appear in featured snippets, voice search results, and direct answers. Uses FAQ schema, clear formatting, and question-based content.

Why it matters: When Google shows a direct answer, that's often the only result people see. AEO helps you be that answer.

Most web designers only do basic SEO. We build every website with all three optimization types built in, so you capture traffic from Google, AI assistants, and voice search.

Why Contractors Need 24/7 Phone Answering

Construction projects are high-value, and homeowners are researching multiple contractors before deciding. When they're ready to request quotes, they call everyone on their list. First contractor to respond and schedule a consultation wins the majority of jobs. Missing that initial call means losing a $20k+ project to a faster competitor.

The reality for general contractors: you're on job sites managing crews, meeting with subcontractors, dealing with inspectors, or handling material deliveries. You can't always answer the phone.

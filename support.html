Common Issues - MCTB App

App not detecting missed calls

Troubleshooting steps:

  • Check that you've granted the app all required permissions (Phone, SMS, Notifications)
  • Ensure the app is not being battery-optimized (Android Settings > Battery > App optimization)
  • Verify your phone's call log is working correctly
  • If issues persist, email us at support@chaoticallyorganizedai.com with your device model

SMS not sending

Common causes:

  • Check if you've hit your daily SMS limit (Free: 5/day, Pro: 25/day, Pro Plus: 100/day)
  • Verify SMS permissions are enabled
  • Confirm the recipient's phone number is valid and can receive SMS
  • Check if your carrier has blocked automated messaging

How to upgrade/downgrade tier

You can manage your subscription through your account settings in the app, or email support@chaoticallyorganizedai.com to request a tier change.

How to cancel

To cancel your subscription:

  • Go to Account Settings in the MCTB app
  • Select "Manage Subscription" and choose "Cancel"
  • Or email support@chaoticallyorganizedai.com with "Cancel Subscription" in the subject line
  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period

Common Issues - Full AI System

AI not answering calls

Check call routing settings and verify your phone system is correctly forwarding calls to the AI line. If you've confirmed routing is correct, contact support immediately at support@chaoticallyorganizedai.com.

CRM integration not syncing

Troubleshooting steps:

  • Verify API credentials are still valid in your CRM
  • Check if your CRM has any service outages or maintenance
  • Look for error messages in your dashboard logs
  • Contact support with specific error details for faster resolution

How to adjust AI responses

AI response training and adjustments require working with our support team. Email support@chaoticallyorganizedai.com with specific examples of calls where the AI didn't respond as expected, and we'll refine the training.

Billing questions

For all billing inquiries, email support@chaoticallyorganizedai.com with "Billing" in the subject line. Include your account details and specific question.

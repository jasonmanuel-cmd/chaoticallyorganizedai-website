Common Questions

The concerns we hear most—and straight answers.

How fast can the AI dispatch an emergency?

Emergency dispatches happen within seconds of the call. Once the AI detects emergency keywords, your on-call technician receives an immediate SMS and follow-up phone call with the customer's details and issue summary.

Can the AI tell if it's a real emergency?

Yes. The AI asks qualifying questions about the severity of the issue. For example, with a leak, it asks if water can be shut off, if there's flooding, and if the leak is active. This helps prioritize true emergencies while still scheduling routine calls.

What if the customer has no water at all?

The AI recognizes "no water" situations as high-priority and can escalate these immediately. It also asks whether the issue is isolated to one fixture or the whole house to help your tech prepare.

How does the AI handle after-hours plumbing emergencies?

The AI answers 24/7, including nights, weekends, and holidays. For emergencies like burst pipes or major leaks, it immediately dispatches to your on-call plumber via SMS and phone call with full customer details.

Can the AI book routine plumbing appointments?

Yes. The AI can access your calendar and book non-emergency appointments directly. It qualifies the issue first, then offers available time slots and sends confirmation texts with appointment details.

Pricing FAQs

What's included in the $1,200 build fee?
The $1,200 build fee covers complete setup and integration of the AI phone system. This includes: discovery call and workflow mapping to understand your services, pricing, and typical call scenarios; AI training on your specific business; integration with your phone system, CRM, and scheduling tools; custom call flows and escalation rules; initial SEO and GEO optimization setup; and team training (typically 30-60 minutes). Setup typically takes 7-14 days from initial consultation to go-live.
Can I cancel the $197/month service anytime?
Yes. The $197/month service has no contract or long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We believe our service should earn your business each month through performance, not lock-in contracts. If the system isn't delivering value, you should have the freedom to make changes.
Do you offer discounts for multi-location businesses?
We can discuss pricing for multi-location businesses on a case-by-case basis. Each location typically has unique service areas, pricing, and workflows that require separate AI training and integration. However, businesses with 3+ locations may qualify for volume pricing on the build fee and monthly service. Contact us to discuss your specific situation and we'll provide a transparent quote based on the scope of work required.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No. The full AI system does not require a contract or long-term commitment. The system requires the one-time $1,200 build fee upfront, but the $197/month service is month-to-month with 30 days notice for cancellation. We believe our service should earn your business through performance, not contractual lock-in.

Why Roofers Need 24/7 Phone Answering

Roofing emergencies happen during storms, after hail damage, and when leaks suddenly appear. Customers with water coming through their ceiling need help immediately and will call every roofer in their area until someone answers. Storm season generates massive call volume that no manual team can fully handle.

The reality for roofing contractors: you're on roofs where answering the phone isn't safe, juggling multiple crews, or dealing with insurance adjusters. After-hours emergency calls go to voicemail. During storm season, you're overwhelmed with inquiry volume and miss high-value jobs.

Our AI answers every call 24/7, qualifies urgency and job type, gathers property and damage details, and routes emergencies immediately. Storm damage inquiries are captured and queued. Roof replacement quotes are scheduled for inspection. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Roofing Call Scenarios We Handle

Emergency Leak Repairs

Active roof leaks, water coming through ceiling, storm damage causing interior damage. AI assesses severity, asks if customer can place tarp/bucket, gathers leak location, and dispatches emergency repair crew with full context.

Typical value: $400-$1,500 per emergency repair

Storm Damage Inspections

Post-storm assessments, hail damage, wind damage, missing shingles. AI captures storm date, visible damage description, insurance claim status, and schedules inspection. Handles high call volume during storm season efficiently.

Typical value: $500-$2,000 inspection fee + replacement project

Roof Replacement Quotes

Full roof replacements, re-roofing, roof age concerns. AI gathers property details (square footage, stories, current roofing material, age), timeline expectations, and schedules on-site inspection for detailed quote.

Typical value: $8,000-$30,000+ per replacement

Roof Maintenance & Repairs

Shingle replacement, flashing repair, gutter issues, vent problems. AI qualifies repair scope, determines urgency (active leak vs preventive), and schedules service visit with appropriate time allocation.

Typical value: $300-$1,200 per repair

Commercial Roofing Projects

Flat roof repairs, TPO/EPDM systems, commercial inspections, large-scale projects. AI qualifies commercial vs residential, gathers building details, and routes to your commercial roofing division with project scope.

Insurance Claims Support

Insurance inspections, claim documentation, supplement negotiations. AI captures claim number, insurance company, adjuster appointment status, and schedules your inspection to coincide with or precede adjuster visit.

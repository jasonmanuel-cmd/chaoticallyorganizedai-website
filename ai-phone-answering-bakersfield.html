Frequently Asked Questions

Does it sound like a robot?

No. We use advanced neural voice synthesis that includes breaths, natural pauses, and varying intonation. Most callers cannot distinguish our AI from a professional, highly-trained receptionist.

What if the AI doesn't know the answer?

The AI is programmed with a "safety valve." If it encounters a query outside its training, it will politely explain that it needs to check with a technician and offer to bridge the call or take a detailed message for immediate callback.

Is this expensive to set up?

We charge a one-time implementation fee to train and integrate the model, followed by a flat monthly subscription that covers all hosting, API costs, and maintenance. It is significantly cheaper than hiring even a part-time receptionist.

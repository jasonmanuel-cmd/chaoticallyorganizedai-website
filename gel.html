GEL: Generative Engine Lift — AI Recommendation Optimization

When customers across the United States ask AI or search engines "Who should I hire?", is your business being recommended? GEL improves how AI systems (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini) and search engines (Google, Maps) surface your business for local, regional, and national queries.

What is GEL (Generative Engine Lift)?

GEL (Generative Engine Lift) is AI recommendation optimization. We align your digital presence — website content, structured data, local profiles, and authority signals — so generative models and search engines cite your business for queries like "best plumber near me", "emergency HVAC repair", and region-specific searches across the United States.

GEL complements traditional SEO by targeting the new vector of discovery: AI-driven recommendations and map-based results. These systems often synthesize answers from multiple sources; GEL ensures the signals they extract favor your business, increasing visibility and conversion rates for organic, local, and AI-driven traffic.

How GEL Works: The Testing Process

We use a systematic three-phase approach to measure and improve your AI visibility:

01 // INJECTION

We test queries across generative models and search engines using nationally and locally phrased prompts: "best HVAC company near me", "emergency plumber in Chicago", "who should I hire for roofing in Phoenix AZ". We use 30-100 variations per industry to capture natural language diversity and regional phrasing.

02 // ANALYSIS

We measure citation frequency, citation rank, and content origin (what paragraph or FAQ the model cited). We correlate these results with local ranking signals (Google Business Profile, on-page schema, authoritative mentions) to identify which assets drive AI recommendations for national and city-level queries.

03 // SCORING

We provide an AI Visibility Score at national, state, and city granularity. This score helps prioritize high-impact changes — schema updates, FAQ additions, localized landing pages, and authority outreach — to quickly move you into top recommendation positions for targeted queries.

GEL vs Traditional SEO: What's the Difference?

Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's algorithm which ranks pages based on backlinks, keyword relevance, technical performance, and user behavior signals. You compete for position 1-10 on search results pages.

GEL optimizes for AI recommendation engines which cite sources based on content structure (answer-first paragraphs, FAQ sections), schema markup (machine-readable data), brand authority signals (mentions, reviews, citations), and conversational relevance (how naturally content answers questions).

The strategies complement each other. Strong SEO helps GEL performance — sites with good traditional rankings have higher baseline AI visibility. And GEL optimization improves traditional SEO through better content structure and schema implementation. The key difference: SEO gets you found when people search Google. GEL gets you recommended when people ask AI.

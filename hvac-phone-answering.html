Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI phone answering work for HVAC companies?

Our AI phone system answers calls 24/7 with natural conversation. It qualifies emergency vs. routine calls, schedules appointments directly into your calendar, captures lead information, and dispatches true emergencies to your on-call technician. The AI is trained on your specific services, pricing, and service areas.

Can the AI handle emergency HVAC calls?

Yes. The AI is programmed to recognize emergency keywords like "no heat," "no cool," "AC not working," or "furnace broken." It immediately escalates these calls to your on-call technician via SMS and phone call, while still collecting the customer's information for your records.

What HVAC software does the AI integrate with?

Our AI integrates with popular HVAC software including ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, and FieldPulse. It can create new customer records, schedule appointments, and log call details automatically. Custom integrations are available for other platforms.
